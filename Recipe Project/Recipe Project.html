How to Make an Apple Pie!

Calories


512 kcal

Servings


8 servings

Time


1h 30min

Difficulty


Medium

Ingredients:

1 recipe pastry for a 9 inch double crust pie

1/2 cup unsalted butter

3 tablespoons all-purpose flour

1/4 cup water

1/2 cup white sugar

1/2 cup packed brown sugar

8 Granny Smith apples - peeled, cored and sliced





Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C). Melt the butter in a saucepan. Stir in flour to
form a paste. Add water, white sugar and brown
sugar, and bring to a boil. Reduce temperature.
Let simmer. Gently pour the sugar and butter
liquid over the apples.
2. Place the bottom crust in a pan. Fill with apples, mounded slightly. Cover with crust.
3. Bake 15 minutes in the preheated oven.
Reduce the temperature to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Continue baking for 35 to 45
minutes, until apples are soft.
